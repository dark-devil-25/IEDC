Session

LinkedIn Bootcamp Workshop Series

A focused workshop on LinkedIn profile building, networking, and professional presentation.

Date and Time

17 July 2025, 12 PM

The programme was conducted as a midday learning session for participating students.

Venue

Computer Lab, Old Block

The computer lab setting helped students follow the concepts in a practical, hands-on way.

Resource Persons

Muhammed Shehood and Mr. Abdul Basith

Muhammed Shehood led the student session, with guidance from Mr. Abdul Basith of the IEDC cell.

Workshop Focus

Helping students create a clearer professional presence online.

The LinkedIn Bootcamp Workshop Series introduced students to the importance of presenting their skills, academic journey, projects, and achievements in a professional format.

Through this session, the IEDC cell encouraged students to treat LinkedIn as more than a social platform: a place to document learning, connect with opportunities, and build confidence for internships, placements, and future entrepreneurial paths.
