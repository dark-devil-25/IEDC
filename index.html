About IEDC

A sharper structure built around how student ideas actually grow.

The IEDC cell helps students move from curiosity to clarity and from clarity to confident action. It creates a space where innovation is not just discussed, but practiced through collaboration, experimentation, and guided learning.

What We Do

We create opportunities for students to identify problems, build solutions, pitch ideas, and strengthen entrepreneurial thinking.

Why It Matters

Students need more than inspiration. They need a system that helps them start, test, improve, and present their work.

How We Support

Through workshops, showcases, team-based challenges, peer learning, and faculty-backed mentoring.

Campus Context

Built within the academic environment of PEEKAY CICS Arts & Science College, Kozhikode.

PEEKAY CICS Arts & Science College is publicly presented by the college as a self-financing institution established in 2013 by the Calicut Islamic Cultural Society, affiliated with the University of Calicut and located at M.G. Nagar, Mathara, Kozhikode.

That setting gives the IEDC cell a strong base for interdisciplinary student participation, practical exposure, and innovation activities that complement classroom learning.
