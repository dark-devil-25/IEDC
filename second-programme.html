Report

Report on LinkedIn Workshop Organized by IEDC Peekay

IEDC Peekay conducted a LinkedIn Workshop for third semester BBA students on 17-07-2025. This session was part of their FYUG curriculum. A total of 28 students participated in the training.

The workshop was facilitated by IEDC team members, who introduced the importance of LinkedIn in today's job market. Topics covered included setting up a professional profile, effective networking techniques, and tips to leverage LinkedIn for internships and career opportunities.

The session was interactive and well-received, with students showing great interest and actively engaging in the activities. As part of the ongoing workshop series, IEDC plans to extend this training to students from all departments in the coming days.
